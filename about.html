About Kang's Bioinformatics

Professor Kang Junho

Keimyung University Dongsan Medical Center

Research Overview

Professor Kang specializes in bioinformatics and computational biology, focusing on analyzing and interpreting complex biological data. His work encompasses genomics, transcriptomics, and multi-omics approaches to uncover insights into various diseases, including cancer.

Current Projects

  • Multi-omics analysis for colorectal cancer prognosis
  • Development of bioinformatics pipelines for NGS data
  • Exploring cancer heterogeneity using spatial transcriptomics
